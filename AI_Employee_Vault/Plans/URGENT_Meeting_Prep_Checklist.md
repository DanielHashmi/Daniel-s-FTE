## PRESENTATION TEMPLATE STRUCTURE

### Slide 1: Title Slide
**Content**:
- Presentation title
- Your name/company
- Date: January 16, 2026
- Client logo (if available)

### Slide 2: Agenda
**Content**:
- Meeting objectives
- Key topics to cover (3-5 items)
- Expected outcomes
- Time allocation

### Slide 3: Executive Summary
**Content**:
- High-level overview
- Key findings or recommendations
- Critical takeaways (3-4 bullet points)

### Slides 4-10: Main Content
**Structure for each topic**:
- Clear headline stating the key message
- Supporting data or evidence
- Visual element (chart, diagram, image)
- 3-5 bullet points maximum per slide
- Consistent formatting

**Suggested Topics** (customize based on meeting purpose):
- Current situation/context
- Analysis or findings
- Challenges or opportunities
- Proposed solutions
- Implementation approach
- Timeline and milestones
- Resource requirements

### Slide 11: Recommendations
**Content**:

- Priority ranking
- Expected impact
- Next steps

### Slide 12: Next Steps & Timeline
**Content**:
- Immediate actions (this week)
- Short-term actions (this month)
- Long-term actions (this quarter)
- Responsible parties
- Key milestones

### Slide 13: Q&A
**Content**:
- "Questions & Discussion"
- Your contact information
- Thank you message

- Prepare to present without slides if needed
- Know your key messages by heart
